Ravioli Recipe

Description:

Ravioli are a type of pasta comprising a filling enveloped in thin pasta dough. This particular recipe takes about 45 minutes to prepare and yields 20 ravioli which is aproximately 4 servings.

Dough ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and a half tablespoon water

Filling ingredients:

  • 1 container riccota cheese
  • 1 package cream cheese, softened
  • half cup shredded mozzarela cheese
  • half cup provolone cheese, shreeded
  • 1 egg
  • 1 and a half teaspoon dried parsley
